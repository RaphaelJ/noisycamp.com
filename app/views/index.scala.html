How do online transactions work?
If the studio owner wishes to accept online payments, musicians will be allowed to settle the reservation amount at the time of the booking using a valid credit card.
The studio owner will then receive that amount directly on their bank account, minus the NoisyCamp service fee (8% for Free plan users, 5% for Plus plan users).
Payouts to the owner's bank account are done daily for Plus accounts and monthtly for Free accounts (on the 15th day of the month). Payments are released for payout 48 hours after the end of the session.
For example, a payment for a session on June 24th will be transferred on June 26th for Plus plan users but on July 15th for Free plan users.
